What Is Health Insurance?

Health insurance plan is a product offered by insurance companies that covers all costs related to medical expenses of the insured. This can be bought for an individual or for an entire family. The term of the plan is ideally fixed at one year and in some cases one can buy the plan for a term of two to three years as well. With health insurance plan in place, one gets entitled to adequate medical help in case of an emergency or in a pre-planned medical expense, without worrying about the financial costs.

Hospitalisation Expenses

Health plan takes care of entire hospitalisation expenses subject to maximum sum assured and terms of the policy. Most plans also cover pre and post hospitalisation expenses.

Cashless Facility

There is a cashless facility available under which you can avail the best medical care at any of the network hospitals. This way the insured can immediately get treatment without having to wait for arranging the funds.

Riders Available

Under certain riders associated with health plans, you get the claim amount on a lump sum basis so you can use it not only for medical expenses, but also to take care of household expenses.

Types of Health Insurance Policies

Basic Health Policy

covers hospitalization expenses in case of Accidents, Illnesses, Surgeries or any other medical emergency, subject to maximum sum insured during the terms of the policy

  • Individual Policy covers only an invidual's health related expenses.
  • Family Floater Policies cover entire family including spouse & children also in some cases includes parents & extended family too under a single umbrella policy.

Top-up Plans

Top-up plans provide the benefit of higher insurance coverage at only a marginally higher cost. These plans come with a deductible or threshold limit, you need the claim above the deductible in a multiple claim to trigger the Sum Insured for plan. This plan is better than the top-up plans.

  • Top-up can also buy as Individual Policy or Family Floater Policy

Different type of Health Insurance Plans

There are different kinds of health plans catering to individual health requirements and lifestyle needs. Insurance companies offer health plans specially designed for special category of people, you can combine any of these plans according to your need or buy separately.

Critical Illnesses Plan

This plan is to provide health benefits for critical illnesses like cancer, paralysis, stroke, heart-related ailments and the likes. Under these plans, the insured is paid the entire sum insured at the time of diagnosis itself rather than waiting for the treatment to get over

Personal Accident Plan

Accidents can cause disability, partial disability resulting in long recovery periods and rehabilitations. This plan gives a lump sum amount & helps to cope up with expenses during this period including hospitalisation expenses and routine household expenses.

Cancer Care Plans

Even though cancer is part of critical illnesses plan, a health policy dedicated only for cancer is more beneficial if the insured has a family history of the said disease. This way one can have access to substantial amount of funds for managing and treating the disease.

Cardiac Care Plan

These are cardiac special health insurance plans since the cost of treatment has risen drastically in last few years. These plans cover all heart-related ailments and surgeries.

Senior Citizen Plan

Senior citizens are the most vulnerable lot, who require expansive medical care that results in massive expenses too. Senior citizen plan can help them get adequate care without burning a hole in the pocket.

Maternity Policies

These are the plans that take care of maternity related expenses including hospitalisation, upto 2 delivery & MTP expenses and cover the new born baby expenses for a certain period of time.

Diabetics Plans

cover the insured for diabetes and its related complications even if the customer is suffering from the disease at the time of taking the policy. It can be covered from day 1 or after a waiting period depending on the plan.
